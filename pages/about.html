About Me

I am a Christian born and raised from Kansas City, MO. I care deeply for others and hope that through my actions and the way I live, I can show people the love of God and His love for His people. At my church, I volunteer as a Social Media Lead and help spread the message of the gospel and create outreach for people looking for a church home. In my free time, I love to study the Bible and play video games. I one day hope to work in my degree field speading the gospel through art. More about my degree below...

I am a graduate from UCM with a study in Fashion Design. I love to create and sew. I created a 5-piece collection twice, one at UCM, and another at JCCC (my community college). I would love to start my own business one day and create clothes that are sustainable and represent my faith.

A quote that I've loved, since a kid, is a quote from the movie Cinderella..

"Have Courage and be Kind."
